Magnus Carlsen

Magnus Carlsen is a Norwegian chess grandmaster. He was born on November 30, 1990. He's a five-time World Chess Champion, the reigning four-time World Rapid Chess Champion, the reigning six-time World Blitz Chess Champion, and the reigning Chess World Cup Champion. He has held the No.1 position in the FIDE world chess rankings since July 1, 2011, and trails only Garry Kasparov in time spent as the highest-rated player in the world. His peak rating of 2882 is the highest in history. He also holds the record for the longest unbeaten streak at the elite level of classical chess.
